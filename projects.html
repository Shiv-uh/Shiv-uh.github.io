Projects

Below are a few of the projects that I have worked on or am currently working on.

Downtime - (May 2020 - Present)

  • A mobile app for iOS and Android to help people coordinate and find things to do with their friends
  • Features an “explore” feature where users can share unique ideas for things to do in their city and town, giving other users access to lesser-known experiences for their own friend group
  • Utilized a business logic component (BLoC) app architecture to ensure scalability as I continue to build on the app's features
  • Currently working on a chat feature and creating a system to filter out user-shared experiences

CookOPS - (February 2020 - April 2020)

  • An online program system that streamlines the planning and hosting of events for 400+ student residents at Cornell’s Alice Cook House
  • Integrates Cook House’s previous Google Calendar planning workflow into CookOPS, enabling event admins to view existing events and automatically push new events created in CookOPS to the house’s Google Calendar
  • Developed a reminder feature allowing event admins to send custom-timed reminder emails to event attendees

Airbnb in San Francisco - (2019)

  • A look into the impact of Airbnb's presence on the city on San Francisco
  • Examination of property maximum night stay constraints and their relationship to the price of a property
  • Visualizations of effects of city policies and property distribution throughout the city
